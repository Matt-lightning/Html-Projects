President's Mansion The President's Mansion on the campus of The University of Alabama in Tuscaloosa, Alabama has served as the residence of university presidents ever since its completion in 1841.
Denny Chimes Denny Chimes is a 115-foot tall tower on the south side of The Quad at the University of Alabama in Tuscaloosa, Alabama and is named after former university president George H. Denny.
Bryant Denny Stadium Bryant-Denny Stadium is the home of the Crimson Tide football team.
