The University of Alabama

Tuscaloosa, Alabama

History

The University of Alabama was founded in 1820 and opened in 1831.

Quick Facts

  • It is the state of Alabama's oldest public university.
  • It is one of 3 campuses in the University of Alabama system.
  • UA offers 70 undergraduate programs through 12 colleges and schools.

Traditions

  • The mascot is an elephant
  • Battle cry is "Roll Tide!"
  • Nickname is "The Capstone"
