Screen Time Recommendations by Age

  • Babies (0-18 months) — None except video chatting with an adult
  • Toddlers (18-24 months) — Less than 1 hour
  • Children (2-5 years) — 0-3 hours
  • Kids and Teens (6-17 years) — 2 hours
  • Teens and Adults (18+ years) — 2-4 hours

Source: All About Vision's chart based on the American Academy of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry recommendations
