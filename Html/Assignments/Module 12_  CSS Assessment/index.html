Yea Alabama

Yea, Alabama! Drown ’em Tide! Every ‘Bama man’s behind you, Hit your stride.
Go teach the Bulldogs to behave, Send the Yellow Jackets to a watery grave. And if a man starts to weaken, That’s a shame! For Bama’s pluck and grit have Writ her name in Crimson flame.
Fight on, fight on, fight on men! Remember the Rose Bowl, we’ll win then.
So roll on to victory, Hit your stride, You’re Dixie’s football pride, Crimson Tide, Roll Tide, Roll Tide!!
